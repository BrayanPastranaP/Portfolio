Edgo

Edgo was born as a personal project in which I seek to advertise in a more personalized way the best services and establishments in the city of Durango.


In edgo you can classify by categories and services to find the best and closest ones.


It consists of creating a landing page that shows all the services they offer so that potential clients can schedule an appointment with them and add this page to a set of similar services.
